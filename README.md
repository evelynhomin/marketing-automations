### 1. Article research, drafting and publishing to Framer

**What it does:** Researches a topic, drafts an article, and publishes it straight to a Framer site, with no manual copy-paste in the middle.

**The problem it solved:** Writing and publishing content by hand is slow, and it's easy to lose an afternoon to formatting and uploading rather than writing. This closes the gap between research and a published page.

**How it works:** Node.js scripts handle topic research, draft generation, and publishing through the Framer API.

**Outcome:** Used to publish 4 articles so far.

### 2. GEO baseline audit report

**What it does:** Measures how often a brand actually shows up when people ask ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity or Google AI Mode questions relevant to their industry.

**The problem it solved:** Most brands have no idea whether they're visible in AI answers at all. Before this, the only option was checking manually, one query at a time, with no consistent record of what was asked or found.

**How it works:** I plan and research the actual queries a brand's customers would ask, upfront and by hand, so the baseline reflects real search behaviour rather than guesses. Automation then captures and organises snapshots across all 4 platforms, and generates the report.

**Outcome:** An accurate, repeatable baseline that can be tracked over time, built on real query research rather than assumptions.

### 3. GEO schema and meta fixing across Webflow

**What it does:** Finds and fixes missing or incorrect structured data (schema) and meta tags across a Webflow site.

**The problem it solved:** A business had no schema markup at all, which makes it harder for AI platforms and search engines to understand what a page is actually about.

**How it works:** Scripts read the site's existing pages via the Webflow API, check what schema and meta tags are missing, and apply the fixes.

**Outcome:** Laid the schema and meta foundations for that business's GEO and SEO work. Still being tracked as the work continues.

---

### 4. Automated article posting to Webflow CMS

**What it does:** Publishes drafted articles directly into a Webflow CMS collection, formatted and ready to go live.

**The problem it solved:** Manually formatting and uploading each article into a CMS eats time that's better spent on research and writing.

**How it works:** Node.js script pushes content into Webflow via its API, matching the site's existing CMS structure.

**Outcome:** Saves around 2 hours of manual work per article.

---

### 5. Monthly website analytics comparison and insights

**What it does:** Pulls a site's analytics month over month and sends out a plain-English summary of what changed and why it matters.

**The problem it solved:** Raw analytics dashboards take time to read and don't tell you what actually matters. This turns the numbers into a short list of insights someone can act on.

**How it works:** Fetches data from Google Analytics and Search Console, compares it against the previous month, and generates an email with the key insights.

**Outcome:** Runs monthly, delivered straight to inbox, no manual pulling of reports required.
